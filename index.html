This research foundation establishes the groundwork for a multi-semester capstone project in computational design practice. Building upon precedent studies from the Summer Colloquium, this proposal frames a critical area of investigation that will guide development through fall and spring terms. The framework functions as a strategic map rather than a fixed destination, positioning emerging computational design work within broader theoretical and practical contexts. Each capstone project integrates technical experimentation with rhetorical argumentation and representational innovation. This proposal will be presented to an external design jury as both a culmination of summer research and a foundation for continued exploration. The document delineates research questions, methodological approaches, and conceptual frameworks that will inform computational experiments, visual strategies, and critical arguments throughout the capstone development process.
